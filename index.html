About Allison


For 8 exciting years, I soared as a flight attendant, embracing the thrill of exploration, diverse cultures, and the ever-changing nature of the aviation industry. It is a profession that:

  • honed my communication skills
  • cultivated my adaptability
  • deepened my passion for customer service

As I navigated the friendly skies, I couldn't help but notice the integral role technology played in modern aviation. This realization ignited a new passion within me, propelling me towards a different kind of journey – one into the world of software development.

I look forward to bringing the same dedication and passion to my software development career that I brought to the skies as a flight attendant. The adventure continues, and I can't wait to see where it takes me next!
